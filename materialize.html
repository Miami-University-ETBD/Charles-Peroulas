Who We Are

Miami University Dining Services is dedicated to providing nutritious and delicious meals to our students, faculty, and staff. We emphasize sustainability and local sourcing in our menu planning.

We strive to offer a diverse selection of meal options while accommodating all dietary restrictions and preferences. From vegan to gluten-free options, we aim to meet the needs of our campus community.

Featured Dining Locations

The Hub Food Court

A central dining location offering various cuisine options, from made-to-order sandwiches to international dishes.

Central Hall Dining

Our main dining hall featuring buffet-style dining with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Café 1809

A cozy café offering coffee, pastries, and grab-and-go meals perfect for busy students.
