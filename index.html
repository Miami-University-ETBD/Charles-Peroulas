Dining Halls

Miami University offers several dining halls across campus, providing students with a variety of meal options. Our dining halls are known for their diverse menus, accommodating various dietary needs and preferences. From made-to-order stations to international cuisines, you'll find something to satisfy your appetite.

Each dining hall features a rotating menu, ensuring that students can enjoy different meals throughout the week. We pride ourselves on using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible, supporting both our community and providing nutritious options for our students.

Cafés

For those looking for a quick bite or a caffeine boost between classes, Miami University has several cafés conveniently located throughout the campus. These cozy spots offer a range of beverages, including specialty coffees, teas, and smoothies, as well as light snacks and pastries.

Our cafés also serve as great study spots or meeting places for group projects. With comfortable seating and a welcoming atmosphere, you'll find these locations perfect for both socializing and getting work done.

Food Courts

Miami University's food courts offer a diverse array of dining options all in one convenient location. These bustling hubs feature multiple vendors, allowing students to choose from a variety of cuisines and meal types. Whether you're in the mood for a burger, sushi, or a fresh salad, our food courts have got you covered.

Food courts are ideal for groups with different tastes, as everyone can find something they enjoy. They also tend to have extended hours, making them perfect for late-night study sessions or early morning breakfasts.

Nutrition Information

We are committed to helping our students make informed dietary choices. Nutritional information for all menu items is available upon request and can be found on our official dining website.
